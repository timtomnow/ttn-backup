You can adjust a backup schedule's timing or app list at any time, or delete one
you no longer need. Because schedules live in your calendar once imported,
there's an extra step to keep the calendar in sync.

## Steps

1. Open the **Schedules** tab from the bottom bar.
2. To change a schedule, tap **Edit** on its card, update the **Name**,
   **Cadence**, **Start date**, **Time**, or **Apps to include**, then tap
   **Save changes**.
3. If you'd already imported this schedule's .ics, tap **.ics** again to
   download the updated file and re-import it so your calendar matches.
4. To remove a schedule, tap **Delete** on its card and confirm.

## What you'll see

Edits show up immediately on the schedule's card, including a refreshed next run
time. Deleting removes it from the list — but note that any reminder already in
your calendar keeps firing until you delete it from the calendar itself.
