Clearing the index empties ttn-backup's own list of backups and app details. The
backup files you've saved to a folder, Files, or iCloud Drive are left
untouched — so you can always re-import them afterwards. This is a reset for the
app's list, not a way to delete your backups.

## Before you start

- Make sure you still have your saved `.json` backup files somewhere, so you can
  rebuild the list later if needed.

## Steps

1. Open the **Settings** tab from the bottom bar.
2. Scroll to the **Danger zone** card.
3. Tap **Clear index**.
4. Confirm when prompted.

## What you'll see

The History list empties and app details reset. Your saved files are unaffected
— bring the list back any time by re-importing them, as in
[Rebuild your backups after a data wipe](/help/rebuild-backups-after-a-wipe).
