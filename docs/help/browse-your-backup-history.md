Every backup you make is listed in History, newest first. From here you can
inspect what a backup contains, save a copy of the file, or clear out ones you
no longer need.

## Steps

1. Open the **History** tab from the bottom bar.
2. Each entry shows its date, the apps it covers, its size, and how it was
   saved. Tap **Open** on any entry.
3. In the **Bundle** view you can:
   - **Restore** an individual app — see [Restore an app from a backup](/help/restore-an-app-from-a-backup).
   - Tap **Download .json** to save a copy of the backup file.
   - Tap **Delete bundle** to remove it.

## What you'll see

**Download .json** saves the raw backup file to your device — handy for emailing
it to another device. **Delete bundle** removes it from the list and, if you've
set a backup folder, deletes that file too; copies you saved elsewhere (Files or
iCloud Drive) are not affected.
