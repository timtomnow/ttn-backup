Sometimes you only want to capture one app — for example, right after making a
big change in it. You can back up a single app on its own.

## Steps

1. Open the **Home** tab from the bottom bar.
2. In the **Per-app status** list, find the app you want.
3. Tap **Backup** next to it.

You can also do this from the **Apps** tab: find the app's card and tap **Backup
now**.

## What you'll see

A confirmation appears noting how it was saved, and that app's **Last backup**
time updates. The single-app bundle is added to your [History](/help/browse-your-backup-history)
just like a full backup.
