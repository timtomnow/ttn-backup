ttn-backup can be installed like a regular app, so it sits on your home screen
and opens full-screen. Installing also helps it work offline.

## Steps

1. Open the **Home** tab from the bottom bar.
2. If your browser supports installing, an **Install as app** card appears. Tap
   **Install** and confirm the prompt.
3. If you don't see the card, use your browser's own option instead — on iPhone
   and iPad, tap the Share button in Safari and choose **Add to Home Screen**.

## What you'll see

ttn-backup appears as an icon on your home screen and opens in its own window
without the browser address bar. The **Install as app** card disappears once
it's installed.
